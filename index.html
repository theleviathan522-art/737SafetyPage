Pilot readiness

What every 737 MAX pilot must complete.

Inadequate pilot knowledge of MCAS contributed to both 2018–19 accidents. That training gap has been structurally closed.

01
Computer-Based Training
A structured suite of CBT modules covering flight control systems, MCAS behavior, and updated emergency procedures — completed before any simulator time.
02
Full-Motion Simulator Sessions
Pilots practice MCAS activation scenarios in a Level D full-motion simulator — the highest fidelity available. Not required before the grounding.
03
Knowledge Assessment
A formal proficiency check verifies understanding of MCAS, AoA sensors, runaway stabilizer procedures, and the updated Airplane Flight Manual.
04
NG-to-MAX Differences Course
Pilots already rated on the 737NG complete a tailored course ensuring no one assumes MAX behavior is identical to the NG without knowing what changed.
